Who are we?

Simon

As you might have guessed, my name is Simon and I'm 31 years old. I've been drinking beer since I was 3 years old... or drinking and drinking I was allowed to taste at that age, but even then I loved the taste.

In Denmark at Christmas, it's tradition to eat Risengrød (a rice porridge served with cinnamon and a spot of butter on top.) I didn't really like the porridge and I still don't but it's served with a light sweet beer called Nisse Øl (Goblin Beer/Christmas beer) with low alcohol. And I could drink a whole case (30/case) of them by myself and is still something I enjoy from time to time. See it in my recommended.

Now I drink beer with friends, at social events, after a long workday... well I rarely say no to beer, unless I'm driving.
